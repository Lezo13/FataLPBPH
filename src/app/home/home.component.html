The Origin Story

Chapters Across Borders: A Mixed Legacy

PHASE I: Foundation

FataL began in the highly engaged Philippines gaming community, focused mainly on competitive Point Blank. The original members established the team's core identity—built on strong teamwork, tactical preparation, and disciplined execution. This foundational period was essential for creating the skills and values that guide our strategy today.

PHASE II: EXPANSION

The team reformed and expanded in the UAE, marking the start of a new chapter focused on international growth. The team's current roster is anchored by veteran players from the UAE, successfully combining the team's historical strategic depth with the excitement and high standards of the Middle Eastern esports ecosystem. This move positions FataL to compete on a wider, global platform.

PHASE III: REBUILD

Following the sudden shutdown of the UAE server, only a small core of devoted players remained. The team started by migrating to the Philippine server, beginning a full rebuild of the FataL legacy from scratch, bringing in new local talent and drawing on international experience to grow stronger and make an impact in the original home region.
